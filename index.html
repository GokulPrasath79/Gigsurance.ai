Anti-Fraud Architecture

Built to survive a Market Crash

500 fake GPS signals won't drain our liquidity pool. Our 5-layer adversarial defense catches fraud rings — without punishing honest workers.

GPS Spoofing

Behavioural GPS Verification

Continuous movement trails — not single coordinates. Real GPS drifts ±3–5m naturally. A spoofed static pin has zero variance. We catch it.

Signal Mismatch

Truth Triangle

GPS + cell tower + platform login must all agree. Cell tower data reflects your physical SIM location and cannot be faked by any mock location app.

Fraud Ring

Social Graph Detection

400 claims in 90 seconds is statistically impossible in a real disaster. We map registration clusters and burst patterns to expose coordinated rings.

False Positives

Innocent-First Design

Flagged claims enter a 2-hour human review queue — never auto-rejected. Honest workers earn a Trust Score that absorbs minor anomalies.
